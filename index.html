Inter-Collegiate Competitions

Date: 08/01/2025 (Wednesday) @ 9am

Venue: Erode Arts and Science College, Rangampalayam

The Department of Political Science has proposed to conduct Inter-Collegiate Competitions in Erode Arts and Science College involving the following events:

Events

S.No Events Participation
1 Quiz Quest Team - 2 members
2 Speech Individual
3 Poster Making Individual
4 Flameless Cooking Team - 2 members
5 Fashion Walk Team - 4 to 8 members

Details of Events

Quiz Quest

Team Size: 2 members per team

Topic: Salient Features of the Constitution

Levels:

  • Level 1: Multiple choice questions (25 questions)
  • Level 2: Connections (Find the answer using the pictures)
  • Level 3: Rapid Fire
  • Finals

Rules:

  • 2 members per team
  • Total number of levels: 3 or more
  • No external help or electronic devices allowed
  • In case of a tie, a tiebreaker level or question will be asked
  • Total of 3 winning teams will be selected

Speech

Topics:

  • இன்றைய வாக்கு நாளைய எதிர்காலம்
  • சமத்துவம் என்பது ஒரு தேர்வு அல்ல அது ஒரு உரிமை

Guidelines:

  • Timing: 5 minutes
  • Relevant content only, no obscene or inflammatory language
  • No political party references
  • No visual aids (slides, videos, or props) allowed
  • The judges' decision is final

Rules:

  • In case of a tie, judges will deliberate and decide
  • Total of 3 winners will be selected

Poster Making

Topic: Importance of Fundamental Rights in Our Lives

Guidelines:

  • Participants must bring their own materials (chart, paints, colors, etc.)
  • Poster must align with the given topic

Rules:

  • Posters must be drawn on charts
  • The judges' decision is final
  • Total of 3 winners will be selected

Flameless Cooking

Topic: Traditional Indian Foods

Guidelines:

  • Team Size: 2 members per team
  • Electric connection will be provided
  • No fire or heat sources allowed
  • Only non-heat electric appliances (blenders, mixers) are allowed
  • Maintain cleanliness and hygiene during the event

Rules:

  • Participants must bring their own equipment and ingredients
  • Pre-cooked ingredients are not allowed unless specified
  • Total of 3 winning teams will be selected

Fashion Walk

Topic: Indian Leaders and Cultural Diversity

Guidelines:

  • Team Size: 4 to 8 members per team
  • Each team gets 5-10 minutes for their performance
  • Teams must provide their own music, costumes, and equipment
  • Makeup and hair styling must be done before the event
  • Walk order will be decided by a random draw

Rules:

  • Maintain professionalism and respect on the runway
  • No inappropriate gestures or costumes
  • The judges' decision is final
  • Total of 3 winning teams will be selected
